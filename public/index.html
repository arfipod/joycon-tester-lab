Experimental hardware proof of concept

Joy-Con WebHID Haptics POC

Test whether official Nintendo Switch Joy-Con L/R controllers can be detected and pulsed from Chrome or Edge through WebHID.

Important: this is not the production Open Bistimulation app, not medical software, and not a therapy tool. It is only a browser hardware experiment. Keep the controllers on a table for the first test and press Stop immediately if behavior is unexpected.
